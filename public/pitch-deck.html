🚗 The Driver Model

Drivers aren't gig workers.
They're co-owners.

Driver churn is the silent killer of every mobility company. Ola/Uber drivers have zero loyalty. VertiCar's competitive moat starts with how we treat drivers.

💰

Full payroll, not gig

₹25,000/month base salary. ESI, PF, paid leave. Predictable income — no surge-chasing, no anxious mornings wondering if they'll earn today.

📈

ESOPs for long-serving drivers

10–15% ESOP pool reserved for drivers. 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff. A driver who stays 4 years gets a real ownership stake. This has never been done in Indian mobility.

🏘️

Community identity

Each driver is permanently assigned to one society. They attend the RWA introduction. Members know them by name. "Raju uncle" doesn't leave — because he's part of the community now.

⚙️ How It Works

WhatsApp as the operating system.

1

Society signs up

We onboard 15–20 families in a society. The driver attends the RWA intro meeting — everyone shakes hands. The WhatsApp group is set up. Community is established before the first ride.

2

Book via WhatsApp

AI concierge handles everything. "Need a car tomorrow 8am to Cyber City" → confirmed, reminder sent. "Need a car in 20 mins" → checks availability, batches with a neighbour going the same way if possible.

3

Ride, billed, done

Fixed membership auto-debits monthly. Per-trip usage billed end of month. No surge, no surprises. Members track usage via WhatsApp. Drivers tracked via GPS — ops visibility without an app.

🤝

The carpooling advantage

Neighbours commuting to the same office park (Cyber City, Golf Course Road) share a ride naturally. Reduces per-family trip cost. Increases car utilisation. Builds the community social layer. Uber cannot do this with strangers.

⏰

Slot management — no conflicts

4 prime-time slots + 4 off-peak slots per family per week. AI handles prioritisation: pre-booked beats last-minute. Prime slots (7:30–10am, 6–9pm) managed like airline seats — limited, reserved, valuable.
