⚙️ How It Works

Three steps. Zero friction.

1

Society Onboards

VertiCar signs up an RWA or a critical mass of 10+ families in a society. We assign a curated car + vetted driver exclusively to that community. The driver attends the RWA meeting — everyone meets face to face.

2

Families Subscribe

Each family pays a flat monthly membership. The shared cost makes it significantly cheaper than daily cabs. A WhatsApp group is set up. Rides are booked by message — AI scheduling handles coordination.

3

Community Rides

Same driver, same car, same faces. School runs in the morning. Office commutes. Evening returns. Grandparent clinic visits. The driver becomes part of the community fabric — trusted like building staff.

🤖

AI-powered WhatsApp scheduling

Route optimisation, ride batching, and schedule conflicts handled automatically — no ops overhead per ride.

🏘️

RWA partnership model

Work with Resident Welfare Associations as channel partners. One deal unlocks 200–500 potential member families instantly.
